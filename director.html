    • RAHEEM AZEEZ

    DIRECTOR

    Raheem Azeez serves as the Director of TAM InfoTech Ltd, a company specializing in the sales of high-quality used laptops and phones imported from around the world, as well as organizing comprehensive IT training for institutions and students. With a Bachelor of Economics from the University of Lagos, earned in December 2012, Raheem brings a wealth of economic expertise to his leadership role, guiding the company through the dynamic IT sector with strategic insight and innovation.

    Economic Expertise Driving Innovation
    As an economist, Raheem possesses a deep understanding of market dynamics, consumer behavior, and global trade trends. This expertise allows him to optimize pricing strategies for the company’s diverse range of used laptops and phones, ensuring competitive offerings for customers. His knowledge also plays a key role in identifying emerging trends in IT training, enabling the development of programs that equip institutions and students with relevant, cutting-edge skills. By leveraging his economic background, Raheem enhances the company’s ability to source cost-effective, reliable products from international markets, delivering value to clients worldwide.

    A Diverse Professional Journey
    Raheem’s leadership is shaped by a rich and varied career. He has worked in child support at Cheshire Home Oluyole, Nigeria (January 2015–July 2019), as resource personnel at Taiwo Adeyanju Center for Persons with Disabilities (January 2016–June 2017), and as a security supervisor at Emirate National Security Service in Dubai, UAE (November 2019–March 2022). These roles honed his skills in leadership, communication, and problem-solving, preparing him to effectively manage the company’s operations since taking the helm in April 2022.

    Leadership and Commitment to Growth
    As Director, Raheem oversees the company’s day-to-day activities, fostering a culture of continuous learning and ensuring the delivery of top-notch IT solutions and training services. His commitment to professional development is evident in his pursuit of certifications in November 2023, including Workplace Hazardous Material Information System (WHMIS), CPR/AED/First Aid, Food Safety, and Crisis Prevention Intervention. He encourages his team to stay updated with the latest technological advancements, reflecting his dedication to maintaining high standards and driving excellence.

    Vision for the Future
    Under Raheem’s leadership, TAM InfoTech Ltd is well-positioned to expand its market presence and continue providing innovative technology services. His economic acumen enables the company to navigate market challenges and seize opportunities, ensuring sustained growth and relevance in the IT industry. Whether it’s sourcing the best used devices from global suppliers or tailoring IT training to meet modern demands, Raheem’s strategic vision keeps the company at the forefront of the sector.
